The University at Albany Finalizes Plans for College of Engineering and Applied Sciences

by Audrey Crom

11 MARCH 2016

The University at Albany has confirmed plans to house their newly announced College of Engineering and Applied Sciences (CEAS) in the Schuyler Building located near the University's downtown campus on Western Avenue. CEAS will encompass 127,000 gross square feet with enough lecture spaces, classrooms, offices, and research facilities for more than 1000 students and 180 faculty members and researchers.

About The Herald

The CNSE Herald is a student-run electronic news source serving SUNY Polytechnic Institute. Operating out of the desk next to NFE 4423, the Herald covers topics pertaining to student interests ranging from local and world news to the latest updates in science. The electronic publishing platform allows students to gain experience with technical and journalistic writing, photography, and web design while staying current with the demand for online media.
