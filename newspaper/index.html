Zika Found to be more Deadly than Originally Thought

Recent studies of the Zika virus are inciting doubt amongst experts on whether mosquitoes serve as the primary vector for infection and spread of the disease to humans. Researchers conducted a study surveying the sequence of genes exhibited by Zika in the past decades and found a display of incongruent sequences between human and mosquito strains.
