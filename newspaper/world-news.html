Devastation in Brussels

On March 22nd, the citizens of Brussels became the victims of an ISIS attack that left 32 people dead and 300 more injured. This is the second major terrorist attack in Europe within the last year. Investigations are currently ongoing as to what transpired and who was involved in the Tuesday morning attack.

Just Who is Merrick Garland?

With the recent death of Supreme Court Justice Antonin Scalia, there is now a hole within the court that must be filled.  President Barack Obama has nominated Merrick Garland, Chief Justice of the DC Circuit Court of Appeals, to fill that role.  If he were to be accepted by the senate, what sort of court official would he become?

On March 21, President Barak Obama visited Cuba, the first sitting president to do so in over 85 years. He was accompanied by the first lady and his daughters in addition to a large congressional delegation of both Republicans and Democrats. Over the two-day visit, the president met with President Raúl Castro to discuss the future of their diplomatic relations.
