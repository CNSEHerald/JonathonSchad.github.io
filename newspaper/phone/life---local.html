NY Legislature Enacts SUNY-wide In-state Tuition Freeze for Next Fiscal Year

This past week, the New York State Legislature and Governor Andrew Cuomo finalized the New York state budget and failed to renew the NY SUNY 2020 legislation, which had previously allowed for a steady, predictable increase in SUNY tuition. The failure of the SUNY 2020 bill to achieve renewal translates to a SUNY and CUNY wide freeze for in-state tuition while still enacting the 10% increase for out-of-state students for this upcoming fiscal year.
