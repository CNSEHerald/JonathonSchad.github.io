About UGSA

The CNSE UGSA was established in 2014 to serve as the voice of the undergraduate students that are located at the Albany site of SUNY Polytechnic Institute. CNSE UGSA works closely with univeristy admistration and the Utica site SA to better the college experience for all students.

Spring 2016 Executive Board Meetings

Posted January 26, 2016 by Jonathon Schad

The CNSE UGSA will be conducting executive board meetings on Fridays starting at 12:30pm. Meetings will be biweekly starting the first week of February. Meetings are open to all CNSE undergraduate students and a open forum is provided at the end of each meeting to address and concerns or questions brought by any students in attendence.

CNSE Laser Tag Event

Posted January 23, 2016 by Jonathon Schad

The CNSE UGSA, in association with the Office of Student Affairs and Residential Life, is planning a night of lasertag on April 6th at Zero Gravity located on Central Avenue in Albany. There is no cost to attend for CNSE students. Transportation will be provided to and from Zero Gravity. Exact time of the event and a link to RSVP will be provided soon.
