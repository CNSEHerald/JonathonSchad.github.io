UGSA Committees

Joint Committee on Undergraduate Student Affairs

Standing Committee

This committee meets monthly with the President and CEO of SUNY Poly as well as other administrative and operational staff to discuss concerns relating to the undergraduate students of CNSE in order to better serve the CNSE students.

Committee Members: UGSA Executive Board, Jonathon Schad (Chair)

Student Affairs Committee (SWAT)

Standing Committee

This committee meets with the Vice President and COO of SUNY Poly to recommend facility improvements to the CNSE campus that would improve quality of the education and student life of CNSE undergraduate students.

Committee Members: Jeff Richards (Chair), Chris Kendra, Dan Kaloustain, Elena Musteata

UGSA-GSA Joint Committee on Clubs and Student Organizations (CCSO)

Standing Committee

CCSO is the committee that approves recognition of student organizations by the UGSA as well as distributing appropriate funds to these organizations from the Student Activity Fee.

Committee Members: Chris Kendra (Chair), William Gasperi

Constitution Committee

Ad Hoc Committee

Reviews the constitution and bylaws of the UGSA and recommends changes to both documents to the UGSA Executive Board.

Committee Members: William Gasperi (Chair), Jonathon Schad, Bart Deprospo

Events Committee

Ad hoc Committee

Plans and executes events that will help foster the community spirit of the CNSE undergraduate student population.

Committee Members: Munzerin Uddin (Chair), Chris Kendra, Aritra Saha

College Assembly Committees

Council on Academic Infrastructure and Information Systems

Standing Committee

Ensures that classroom space at CNSE is up-to-date and in functional order to ensure the effectiveness of the classes being taught in the those spaces.

Committee Members: Chris Kendra

College Life Council

Standing Committee

Looks at CNSE student concerns to make sure they are being properly addressed by the administration.

Committee Members: Mike Derevensky, Daniel Kaloustian, Elena Musteata

Council on Research

Standing Committee

Talks about ongoing research at CNSE and what improvements can be made to increase research.

Committee Members: William Gasperi

Council on Student Recruitment

Standing Committee

Discusses recruitment methods being used to bring talent to CNSE's bachelor's degree programs.

Committee Members: Mike Derevensky, Jordan Carter

Environmental Health and Safety Council

Standing Committee

Ensures that all students have the proper training to access labs on campus and that training programs are comprehensive.

Committee Members: Jeff Richards

Educational Outreach and Internships Council

Standing Committee

Looks at how CNSE interacts with the community and promotes STEM principles including the onsite children's museum and CNSE summer internship program.

Committee Members: Jonathon Schad, Daniel Kaloustian

Undergraduate Academic Council

Standing Committee

Reviews current and proposed classes to ensure they are providing the maximum benefit to CNSE students.

Committee Members: Jonathon Schad

Governance Council Committees

Governance Council

Standing Committee

The mission of the SUNY Poly governance unit is to participate in governance at the federal level and to ensure the separate governance units at the Utica and Albany sites work together fruitfully and harmoniously in support of the faculty and students and SUNY Poly’s education, research, and service objectives.

Committee Members: Jeff Richards

Committee on Libraries, Technology, and Distance Learning

Standing Committee

1. Review and make recommendations with respect to the technological and academic infrastructure, including but not limited to the library, computing, distance learning, and presentation systems, and policies and procedures associated with the use of technology in the institution’s educational programs.

Committee Members: GSA Appointee - Andrea Leonardi

Committee on Academic Assessment

Standing Committee

1. Review the policies and procedures adopted at each SUNY Poly site to review that site’s academic programs, including majors, minors, and General Education. Ensure that each site’s policy and procedure is in accord with SUNY, SED and MSCHE requirements and expectations.

Committee Members: William Gasperi

Committee on SUNY Poly Planning, Policy and Budget

Standing Committee

1. Advise the SUNY Poly administration on matters of institution-wide mission and vision.

Committee Members: GSA Appointee - Karsten Beckmann

Committee on Acadmic Affairs

Standing Committee

1. The Academic Affairs Committee addresses all issues that are part of the faculty's primary responsibility that are not delegated to other standing committees of the Governance Council. These issues include, as they are relevant at SUNY Poly rather than merely constituent site level, admissions and transfer policy, the academic code of conduct, academic freedom, academic standing, grading, graduation requirements, research, subject matter and methods of instruction, and those aspects of student life which relate to the educational process.

Committee Members: Michael Derevensky
